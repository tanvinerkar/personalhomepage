Hi, I'm Tanvi Nerkar

Freshman Undergraduate Student

I am an undergraduate freshman pursuing a Bachelor of Technology degree, with a major in Electrical Engineering, at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur.

An avid reader, a passionate coder and an earring fanatic, I am the quintessential example of a college student working to find her way in the world.

I believe that each of us can make a difference to the world by contributing to it in our own way, be it small or big. I'd like to do the same by spreading knowledge about the things I love and getting to learn so much more myself.

Technology is the way ahead for us as human beings. As the first generation to be brought up surrounded by advancing developments in technology, we are honour bound to ensure that growth in tech continues to happen at an exponential pace.

I would like to be the harbinger of something new, something innovative, something that has never been thought of before. In order to do so, I would like to interact with as many people as possible, learning new qualities from each and every one of them. So, if you are reading this, do reach out to me about any topic you wish to speak about. I am an absolute extrovert, so feel free to talk to me!
